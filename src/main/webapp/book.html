I'm Beth. I've read Harry Potter every year since it's come out. It's long been a goal of mine to buy paperbacks so I can write in them and not ruin my nice hardback copies. This is where I come to spazz about Harry Potter. All the book text belongs to JK Rowling. All the notes/questions/insights are mine. *Please note that I have a complete set of hardbacks I haven't written in, and that I bought a set of paperbacks for this purpose before you yell at me for writing in the books. Thank you. Everyone who has reblogged one of my posts to comment about how awful it is to write in books has missed the point of this blog.* “Print will never die. There’s no substitute for the feel of an actual book. I adore physically turning pages, and being able to underline passages and not worrying about dropping them in the bath or running out of power. I also find print books objects of beauty.” ~JK Rowling
